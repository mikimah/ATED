Dear Scientists and Researchers

We are honored to invite you to the ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE, TECHNOLOGY, ENGINEERING & DESIGN. Which will be held in Vietnam, Saigon Technology University between on October, 2026. The congress is organised with the theme of "ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE, TECHNOLOGY, ENGINEERING &" DESIGN with the aim of presenting studies in many disciplines

It aims to bring together scientists working on subjects such as engineering, architecture, art history, archaeology, art history and archaeology with traditional and contemporary approaches on an international scale, to present and share their valuable researches on related subjects and to bring them together on a common platform. It is also aimed to emphasise the importance of issues such as the professional training of actors who are effective in the field of architecture and engineering and design, and technological developments in the field.
